# Play Console — Content rating questionnaire (AlbaBiz.ie)

Category to choose: **Utility, Productivity, Communication, or Other** →
"Reference, News, or Educational" / **Business directory**. AlbaBiz is a
business directory; none of the sensitive-content questions apply.

Answer the IARC questionnaire as follows (all **No** unless noted):

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Violence (cartoon, fantasy, realistic) | No |
| Blood / gore | No |
| Sexual content / nudity | No |
| Profanity / crude humor | No |
| Use of alcohol, tobacco, or drugs | No |
| Gambling (simulated or real) | No |
| Horror / fear themes | No |
| User-generated content / user interaction | **Yes** — users can submit a business listing (moderated by ACAI before it appears). |
| Shares user location | **No** — the app does not collect or share device location. Business addresses are entered manually by submitters. |
| Digital purchases | No |

### Notes on "user-generated content"

- Submissions are **moderated**: every listing is reviewed by an ACAI admin
  before it becomes public (status starts as `pending`).
- There is **no** open chat, comments, or messaging between users.
- A **removal / report** mechanism exists (`/hiq`) and is processed by admins.

Expected result: **rated for everyone / PEGI 3 / ESRB Everyone**, possibly with
a "Users interact" notice due to the moderated submission feature.

### Target audience & content

- Target age group: **18+** (it's a business tool; not directed at children).
- Not designed for or appealing primarily to children → answer the
  **Families / child-directed** questions as **No**.
